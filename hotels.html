Hotels

Luxury Hotels

  • The Dylan Amsterdam: Situated in a historic canal-side building, this boutique hotel offers chic and individually designed rooms, along with a Michelin-starred restaurant.
  • Waldorf Astoria Amsterdam: Located along the picturesque Herengracht canal, this luxury hotel offers elegant rooms, a Michelin-starred restaurant, and a beautiful garden terrace.

Budget-Friendly Hotels

  • Hotel Van Gogh: A budget-friendly hotel within walking distance of the Van Gogh Museum and other attractions. It offers clean and simple rooms.
  • The Flying Pig Downtown Hostel: A popular choice among backpackers, the hostel offers a vibrant atmosphere and dormitory-style accommodation.

Boutique Hotels

  • The Hoxton Amsterdam: This stylish boutique hotel is known for its cozy rooms, lively lobby, and on-site restaurant.
  • Pestana Amsterdam Riverside: A boutique hotel set in a former town hall. It offers elegant rooms, a riverside terrace, and a fitness center.

Canal View Hotels

  • Pulitzer Amsterdam: With its canal-side location, this hotel offers classic and comfortable accommodation in a historic setting.
  • Ambassade Hotel: Nestled along the Herengracht canal, this hotel boasts a charming atmosphere and rooms with picturesque canal views.

Remember that Amsterdam is a popular tourist destination, so it's a good idea to book your accommodation well in advance, especially during peak travel seasons.
